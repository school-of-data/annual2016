Network
Lab

Read more: schoolofdata.org/



In 2016, we launched Network Lab, a seed-funding scheme designed to enable members to pursue innovative projects, strategically important to the growth of the network as a whole. Our call for proposals went out to individual and organisational members, as well as existing local implementation partners.

Of the six grantees this year, projects included a data visualisation card game, a radio series about data literacy, a project about data literacy in Brazilian favelas, a project about Education Data Advocacy in Colombia, a tool to search multiple ckan instances (to be used during the "FIND" step of data expeditions), and a series of templates to better evaluate data literacy workshops.

With this new programme, we hope to allow our members to bring data literacy to their localities in engaging, fresh and innovative ways.







What School of Data does is core to Open Knowledge International and we fully support the distributed model going forward.
Pavel Ricther,
CEO, Open Knowledge
