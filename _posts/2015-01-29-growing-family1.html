OUR GROWING SCHOOL OF DATA FAMILY





STEERING COMMITTEE

At our third annual Summer Camp, the School of Data network members elected a new Steering Committee. This new committee will preside for two years. Along with overseeing the budget, strategy and sustainability of the School of Data project, the new Steering Committee will oversee the formation incorporation of School of Data into an entity independent from Open Knowledge International. The newly elected members are Bardhyl Jashari, Director of Metamorphosis Foundation (Macedonia); Pavel Richter, CEO at Open Knowledge International; Camila Salazar, former Fellow and data journalist at La Nacion, Costa Rica; Juan Manuel Casanueva of Social TIC; and Sylvia Fredriksson, designer and project coordinator at École de Données (School of Data France). We welcome every new Steering Committee member and wish them every success in their post!





NEW MEMBERS





SWIZTERLAND

OPEN KNOWLEDGE SWIZTERLAND

Open Knowledge Switzerland formally joined our network in 2016 and we are very happy to have them join us! As well as facilitating regular, local hackathons in Switzerland, the group offers workshops on developing data skills for civic engagement, and using open data more critically and creatively.





LATVIA

DATU SKOLA

It was thanks to Nika Aleksejeva, our School of Data 2016 Fellow, that a group in Latvia joined our network this year. Through Nika’s fellowship activities, she was able to raise awareness of the importance of data literacy in her country and a community was established soon afterwards. Already since forming in October, the group has completed some activities around the Latvian Open Data Portal and organised its first hackathon, addressing topics including how the pharmaceutical industry impacts health care in Latvia and an analysis of variations in teacher salaries in the country.





Nigeria

Connected Development

We are thrilled to officially welcome Connected Development to the School of Data family. School of Data has been working with Connected Development since 2014 when their co-founder, Oludotun Babayemi, became a 2014 School of Data Fellow. Since, they have continued to actively participate in the School of Data network supporting our fellows in Nigeria, organising their annual Open Data Party and advising local civil society groups on the use of data. In 2016, they participated in the Network Innovation Lab, receiving funds to launch the School of Data Radio. Connected Development is a leading civil society organisation with expertise in organising trainings and data literacy workshops, campaigning and community building.

School of Data Radio in Action !




GERMANY

DATEN SCHULE

Open Knowledge Germany is an independent not-for-profit based in Berlin, Germany. Their work is designed to help citizens obtain critical information and to enable informed decision making. They seek to generates societal and economic value through making data available and useful. They strive to make open knowledge an integral component of the modern world, online as well as offline. They joined the School of Data network in 2016 through the official launch of Daten Schule. The programme is designed to be an educational programme that teach everything from data collection, analysis and visualization to support CSOs to do data - based campaigning. The project involves a combination of workshops, strategy consulting and technical training. in 2016, Daten Schule received the Google Impact Challenge! We are pleased to welcome Open Knowledge Germany and Daten Schule to our family.
